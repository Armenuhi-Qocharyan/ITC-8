Photo editor
--------------

Functionality
-------------
    * show photo
    * draw circle on photo
    * to grey photo 
    * to gray photo custm
    * to binary photo
    * to binary photo custom

How run program
----------------
    Go to directory programm:
        * cmake CMakeLists.txt
        * make 
        * ./actImages
